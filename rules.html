About Game

Higher or Lower is a simple guessing game based on the card game from the classic tv shows ‘Strike it Lucky’ and ‘Play your cards right’. Your goal is to guess if the next number is going to be ‘higher’ or ‘lower’. If you guess correct then you gain a point and can guess the next number. Simple and fun! The more you guess correctly the more you score.

How to play

When the number is shown:

Keyboard

H or Up arrow = Higher
L or Down arrow = Lower
